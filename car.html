Family Cars

A family car is a vehicle designed to meet the needs of an average family. Typically, these cars can comfortably accommodate multiple passengers and provide sufficient storage space. They are usually hatchbacks or sedans but can also include other body styles like minivans (MPVs), SUVs, and crossovers.

Sport Cars

A sports car is a vehicle designed for performance driving, characterized by a high-powered engine, sleek and aggressive styling, and superior handling capabilities. Typically, these cars prioritize speed, agility, and an exhilarating driving experience over practicality
